Pirate is a social music app built around publishing, discovery, chat, and learning.

You can browse a feed, manage a profile, message over XMTP, listen to music, and work through language-learning flows powered by songs and short-form content.

The Android app also includes:

- Tempo passkey-based account access
- music playback and playlist flows
- artist and profile pages
- chat with the Pirate assistant
- study and streak features

Authentication uses Android Credential Manager. Passkey support depends on the device, Android version, browser or app integration, and installed credential provider. On some GMS-free setups, passkey flows may be limited or unavailable.
